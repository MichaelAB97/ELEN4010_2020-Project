Splitting Bills

In a 'cleaver' way

BillCleave is a new interactive application that provides you with a new modern day approach of the IOU system. With the user house features, you will be able to track your expenses and know which person in your house owes you money as well as the people you owe.

Features

Add, join or leave a house.

Add expenses to the each house you are a member of

View each member of the house and their contact details

View all the transactions of the house and their statuses.
